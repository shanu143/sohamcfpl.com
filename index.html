About the Company

Welcome to the dynamic world of Soham Clearing & Forwarding Pvt. Ltd one stop destination for all your logistic needs. We are among the leading Freight Forwarding Services providers, incepted in India. Our authentic and trusted services have helped us to achieve an unrivalled name for ourselves. With the help of our vast and well settled network throughout India and world wide, we are able to provide our customers with hassle free services for Customs Clearing services as well as other Allied Logistics Services including Air and Sea freight forwarding, Surface Transport, Warehousing
more..

Vision

To create a world class supply chain brand which will offer the most simplified, innovative, reliable and seamless supply chain solutions with real-time visibility along the supply chain pipeline to customers so as to free them to concentrate on their core businesses competencies and help them grow for mutually beneficial long term business relationship.

Mission

To extend the fundamentals of our core competency of "Ability and Reliability" factor to all our existing services verticals and seamlessly synergise them with the other evolving service modules in creating a World Class Seamless Supply Chain Brand.
